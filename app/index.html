Who we are

Christoph is the frontend team lead at CouchCommerce and the founder of the HannoverJS meetup. He began with programming at the age of 10 with BASIC but has since moved on to become proficient in various different programming languages and technologies.

Christoph is also the creator of several popular open source projects including grunt-html-snapshot and SignalR.Reactive

Ever since he discovered git he spent countless hours getting into the nitty gritty details of the popular version control system and loves spreading the joy of it.

Pascal works as a Software Engineer at CouchCommerce and is a Co-Founder of thoughtram. He loves contributing to open source projects and speaks at conferences and meetups. He is also a big fan of animated gifs.

During his apprenticeship, he already gave lectures about web technologies in school. He loves it when teaching people things and the penny drops.

Pascal created the famous angular-translate module to help people bringing i18n to their AngularJS apps.
